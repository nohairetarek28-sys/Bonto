# Bonto - Tactical Pro ⚽👑

Welcome to **Bonto**, a tactical football card game where strategy beats luck! 


## 🎮 What is this game?
Bonto is a turn-based strategy card game inspired by football (soccer). Your goal is to score more goals than your opponent before the 20-minute timer runs out. You manage your hand, deploy defenders and attackers on the pitch, and use legendary players with unique special abilities to crush your opponent.

## 📜 How to Play
- **Moves:** You start each turn with 3 moves. Placing a card on the field costs 1 move.
- **Attacking:** Select 1 or 2 attackers from your field to strike. Attacking costs moves based on the number of attackers.
- **Support Cards:** During an attack, a random "Bonto" card is drawn automatically to support your base attack power.
- **Legends 🌟:** You have legendary cards like Pele, Zidane, Ronaldinho, and Yashin. To play a legend, you must sacrifice (burn) 2 cards from your hand as a tax.
- **Hand of God:** If you attack with Maradona, you must guess the type of the support card that will be drawn. If you guess right, it's an instant goal!

## 🚀 Features
- Custom interactive UI with a tactical football pitch.
- Smart opponent AI that defends and counter-attacks.
- Special abilities for Legend cards (e.g., Yashin blocks all legend powers, Zidane adds magic power, Ronaldinho bypasses defenders).
- Built purely with HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript.
